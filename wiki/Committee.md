# Committee Info

We're looking to become a Committee of Inter-Actief. GameCie managed to put
together an amazing event for Inter-Actief GameJam 2025, but their interests
currently lay elsewhere.

JackCie is looking to continue the legacy of both the GameJam and Hackathon
committees.
